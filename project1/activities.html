Nile River Cruises

Experience Egypt the way the pharaohs did by sailing the legendary Nile River. A cruise along the Nile is not just a journey through space but also through time, offering glimpses of life that has remained unchanged for millennia alongside ancient monuments.

Most cruises operate between Luxor and Aswan, with stops at major temples and archaeological sites along the way. Enjoy the comfort of your floating hotel while witnessing rural Egyptian life along the riverbanks.

Highlights:

  • Visit the temples of Karnak, Luxor, Edfu, and Kom Ombo
  • Witness stunning sunsets over the Nile
  • Experience traditional Nubian entertainment
  • Enjoy gourmet dining while cruising

Best time: October to April when temperatures are milder

Desert Safari Adventures

The vast Egyptian deserts offer incredible adventures for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike. From the Western Desert with its stunning White Desert and Black Desert to the Eastern Desert mountains, Egypt's arid landscapes are breathtakingly beautiful.

Tours range from day trips to multi-day expeditions, allowing you to experience Bedouin culture, observe unique geological formations, and witness extraordinary starry nights in the desert silence.

Popular Desert Activities:

  • Camel trekking through golden sand dunes
  • Quad biking and dune bashing
  • Camping under the stars with Bedouin guides
  • Visiting desert oases like Siwa, Bahariya, and Dakhla
  • Sandboarding down massive dunes

Safety note: Always go with experienced guides and tour operators

Red Sea Diving & Snorkeling

The Red Sea coastline offers some of the world's most spectacular underwater experiences. With crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and abundant marine life, Egypt has become a premier destination for diving and snorkeling enthusiasts.

Popular coastal resorts like Sharm El Sheikh, Hurghada, and Dahab provide access to breathtaking dive sites suitable for all experience levels, from beginners to advanced divers.

Underwater Highlights:

  • The Blue Hole in Dahab (for experienced divers)
  • Ras Mohammed National Park's coral gardens
  • Thistlegorm wreck – one of the world's most famous dive sites
  • Colorful fish, dolphins, turtles, and occasionally reef sharks

Available year-round with water temperatures ranging from 21°C to 28°C

Family-Friendly Activities

Egypt offers plenty of engaging activities for travelers of all ages:

  • Camel rides around the pyramids - A classic experience that children love
  • Sound and light shows - Entertaining historical narratives at the pyramids, Karnak Temple, and Philae Temple
  • Felucca sailing - Relaxing traditional sailboat rides on the Nile
  • Beach resorts - Family-friendly accommodations with pools and beaches in Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh
  • Interactive museum exhibits - The new Grand Egyptian Museum has sections designed specifically for children
