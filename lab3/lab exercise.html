Top Museums To Visit in New York City


Iconic Museums of New York City

Metropolitan Museum of Art:

The Metropolitan Museum of Art, commonly known as the Met, is an encyclopedic art museum located in New York City. It is the largest art museum in the Americas and the fourth-largest in the world, housing over 5,000 years of art from various cultures and regions. In 2023, it welcomed 5.36 million visitors, making it the most-visited museum in the United States. The museum features extensive collections, including Egyptian, European, American, and Islamic art, among others.


American Museum of Natural History:

The American Museum of Natural History is located on the Upper West Side of Manhattan in New York City. It features 45 permanent exhibition halls and houses around 35 million specimens, covering topics from dinosaurs to outer space. The museum is open daily from 10 am to 5:30 pm, and visitors are encouraged to reserve tickets online for faster entry. It is a popular attraction, known for its extensive collections and educational programs.


Museum of Modern Art:
The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York City is one of the most influential institutions for modernist art in the world. It features a vast collection of modern and contemporary artworks, including paintings, sculptures, photography, and design.


New York Transit Museum:
The New York Transit Museum is a museum that displays historical artifacts of the New York City Subway, bus, and commuter rail systems in the greater New York City metropolitan region. The museum is located in an actual subway station, which was originally called Court Street. Visitors can board the vintage cars, sit at the wheel of a city bus, step through a time tunnel of turnstiles, and explore changing exhibits that highlight the cultural, social and technological history, and future , of mass transit.
